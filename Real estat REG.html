Buyer/User Account

This individual account enables you to search for properties, save properties for later viewing, get alert for saved searches and gives you direct contact to the sellers.

Buyer/User Account

This individual account enables you to search for properties, save properties for later viewing, get alert for saved searches and gives you direct contact to the sellers.

Seller's Account

This individual account allows properties' owners list their properties themselves and gives them full control over how to market and get their buyers.

Agent's Account

This is an individual Agent's account for professionals in real estate. This account enables an agent to list properties under his/her mandate either as an independent marketer or as an agent under a named agency..

Agency Account

This account is for real estate company that list properties on behalf of clients/sellers. Agents are registered under agency, giving the agency control of the various agents and agents' listing. The agency has the mandate of all properties under the agency.
